How Predictive AI Enhances Telehealth

Telehealth has emerged as a vital tool for managing chronic diseases like COPD. With wearable sensors and real-time respiratory monitoring, patients can be observed remotely without needing frequent hospital visits.

Wearable Acoustic Sensors

These sensors detect sounds like wheezing or crackles that may indicate worsening symptoms. Signals are converted into data and analyzed by AI models to detect abnormal breathing patterns.

Predictive Algorithms in Action

AI algorithms (such as neural networks and support vector machines) can predict potential exacerbations by interpreting data from wearable devices. This enables:

  • Early warning for patients and doctors
  • Reduced emergency visits
  • Improved long-term outcomes

Real-World Results

Studies using the ICBHI dataset show over 80% accuracy in detecting symptoms from respiratory sounds, demonstrating how AI can provide a reliable early-warning system for COPD.
