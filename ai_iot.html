Overview

The combination of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies has opened new possibilities in remote health monitoring, especially in managing chronic illnesses like COPD.

IoT in Healthcare

  • Wearable sensors (e.g., smart inhalers, acoustic breath monitors)
  • Continuous data collection from home environments
  • Automated data transfer to healthcare providers

Role of AI

  • Analyzes large volumes of patient data
  • Detects early signs of exacerbations through pattern recognition
  • Supports clinical decision-making

Benefits of Integration

Integrating AI and IoT enables proactive care by alerting patients and doctors before exacerbations become severe. It also empowers patients to take greater control over their health.
